My New York Trip

In 2017 I took a trip to New York. I went to all the big cities, checked out all the landmarks, and more. My favorite part of the trip was by far going to New York City, specifically going to Times Square. Standing in the middle of Times Square I found myself frozen in time. Buildings as tall as you can imagine, with screens bigger then you've ever seen, roads spanning for what looked like forever. It was quite honestly surreal. Other places I went to was Albany, Syracuse, The American Museum of Natural History, and Central Park.
